Trip Details

Gentle Rapids Family Adventure: Perfect for families with children ages 6 and up. This 3-hour journey takes you through Class I-II rapids with plenty of calm water for swimming and relaxation. Our experienced guides will teach you basic paddling techniques while you enjoy stunning canyon scenery. Includes safety equipment, professional instruction, and riverside snacks. The trip concludes with a gentle float through our most picturesque section of river.

Adventure Rapids Experience: Our most popular full-day adventure! This 6-hour expedition combines thrilling Class III rapids with breathtaking wilderness views. Navigate through challenging boulder gardens, surf standing waves, and experience the rush of "Big Kahuna" and "Washing Machine" rapids. Includes a delicious riverside lunch, all safety gear, and professional photography service. Minimum age 12 years. Previous rafting experience recommended but not required.

Extreme White Water Challenge: For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush! This 8-hour expedition tackles Class IV-V rapids including the legendary "Satan's Cesspool" and "Terminator" drops. Only for physically fit participants with previous Class III+ experience. Includes technical gear briefing, advanced safety protocols, and celebration dinner. Minimum age 18 years. Weather and water conditions must be optimal. Limited to 6 participants per guide for maximum safety.
